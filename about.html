About Medicine Tracker

Welcome to the Medicine Tracker website. This web app is designed to help you efficiently manage your medication schedule by allowing you to add medicine names, dosages, and the times you need to take them.

Our goal is to provide a simple, user-friendly tool that saves you time and helps you never miss a dose. The app stores your information securely in your browser and offers easy-to-use controls to add or remove medicines whenever you want.

Built using modern web technologies such as HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, this website demonstrates core functionalities for tracking medicines and managing health routines.
