Frequently Asked Questions

What is Medicine Tracker?
Medicine Tracker is a web app that helps you manage and track your medication schedule, set reminders, and stay healthy.
How do I add my medicines?
Use the Add Medicine form on the homepage or in your profile section to list your medicines, doses, and intake times.
How can NGOs get involved?
NGOs can partner with us by registering through the NGO section and collaborating on healthcare outreach programs and free medicine drives.
How do I become a donor?
Visit the Donor page to register your details and specify the type of medicine or funds you wish to donate.
Is my data secure?
Yes, all your information is securely stored locally on your device, and we do not share data without consent.
Can I edit or delete my medicine details?
Currently, you can delete existing entries. Editing functionality will be added in future updates.
